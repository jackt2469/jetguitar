About

Hi, I’m Jack Taylor, a guitarist from Surbiton with nearly 20 years of experience. My main passions lie in playing blues, classic rock, and metal, but I’m always eager to explore new genres and musical styles.

Since 2011, I’ve been a guitarist in the metal band Bangover, touring across the country and releasing several albums and EPs. For our latest album, I took on the role of home recording and producing, which was an incredible learning experience that fueled my passion even more. This journey inspired me to share my knowledge and skills through teaching and creating great music.

All my services are tailored to your personal taste—I want you to learn and record the music you love, not just practice songs you’re indifferent about. This approach made my own learning enjoyable and effective, and I believe it will do the same for you.

If this sounds like something you’d be interested in, please reach out using the contact details at the bottom of the site.

Thanks for stopping by!

'Track in Ten' Course


Track in Ten is a 10-week, one hour per session course designed for complete beginners to learn the fundamentals of guitar—starting from the very basics and progressing all the way to writing and recording your first original song.

This course will be completely tailored to your music style, whatever that may be, and we can discuss this in our initial consultation.

Course Overview:

Weeks 1–6 – Learning to Play

Week 1: Guitar basics – posture, tuning, holding the pick
Week 2: Strumming and rhythm – timing, playing with a metronome
Week 3: Major & minor chords, reading guitar tabs, playing basic songs
Week 4: Keys and scales – intro to melody
Week 5: Song structure – explore new songs with more interesting chords
Week 6: Power chords & barre chords

Weeks 6–8 – Writing & Recording

Week 6: Begin creating your own song – structure and rhythm
Week 7: Continue writing – add a lead melody, intro, and solo
Week 8: Recording session (1 hour) – track your song

Weeks 9–10 – Buffer Weeks

These can be used to:

– Spend extra time on any area that needs attention (most likely continuation of the recording process)
– Add vocals, bass, or even drums to your track
– Finish or polish your recording

Each Week Includes:

– A PDF with course material and exercises
– Support via WhatsApp or video call if you need clarification

Note: To get the most from the course, you'll need to practice outside of our lessons. One hour a week won't be enough—you'll need to put in time between sessions.

Pricing:

£20 per lesson (paid in cash/card before each session, or via bank transfer/PayPal)

£180 full course (10% discount when paid upfront for all 10 lessons)

Your finished track will be delivered in your preferred format, with all project assets included if requested.
